org.opensha2.geo

Class Bounds

  • java.lang.Object
    • org.opensha2.geo.Bounds

  • public final class Bounds
extends Object
    A rectangular (in Mercator projection) bounding box specified by a lower-left coordinate (min()) and an upper-right coordinate (max()).

    Bounds are 2-dimensional in that the depth component of the corners will always be 0.
